Side note(from personal experience):

SLEEEP IS SUPER FUCKING IMPORTANT - no, you don't thrive off 6 hours #getyour8

As creators it's super easy to get consumed in a project and want to never stop until it's done. The problem with this is that you push yourself too hard one day and then you have to recover with excess sleep the next day. A healthy alternative is to try to focus on getting the right amount of sleep each night and try to maintain the same amount of productivity each day. In the long run the person who is consistent everyday with a strong amount of input out produces the person expending, exhausting, and recovering for longer. Consider this Joe Rogan podcast on working out smarter, the same principle applies to here.

Also to reiterate - check this brief study published by Harvard Health
